Who am I | What do I do

Fawzia Bheekhun is an Integrative Psychotherapist, transformational life coach, speaker, project coordinator for Refugee Council, and a registered clinical supervisor for trainee and qualified psychotherapists. Fawzia is the founder of ‘Emotional Wellbeing’ through which she provides psychotherapy and coaching services and workshops.

With over 12 years’ experience in the field of psychotherapy, she is a leading authority in both male and female cases of human trafficking, torture victims, war victims, domestic abuse, sexual abuse, and psychological abuse. Her projects include The Regional Refugee Project and The Epione Project.

Expertise | Experience

Fawzia is a BACP qualified psychotherapist and certified Transformational Life Coach, she has acquired many certifications and achievements throughout her career.

She has worked with highly respected organisations such as The Refugee Council – one of the UK’s leading charities working directly with refugees and asylum seekers, supporting them to rebuild their lives; Solace Women’s Aid – an independent charity providing life-saving support to over 11,000 women and children; and Mind – a mental health charity supporting anyone experiencing mental health issues whilst also raising awareness and understanding.

Vision | Mission

The vision is to contribute to ‘human empowerment’, to empower both genders that regardless of your past experiences, mental health issues or background you are a worthy of receiving love, confidence, respect, education and opportunity. That each and every one of us deserve the life that we envision.

The mission is to provide key services to individuals suffering with mental health issues, grow global awareness and change the culture and mindset society has towards mental health.

Transformational Life Coaching

I provide one to one coaching that allows each client to have a space for their whole existential self, a chance to stop and pause in that moment to focus the mind and body and have that moment of deep self- realisation about what are the obstacles in all areas of life.

The coaching offered allows the time and space to be heard and listened to with compassion and empathy in a non-judgemental coaching relationship that provides humility and integrity for transformational change to encompass new beginning in one’s life.

I have a holistic approach to coaching where I will design a bespoke way of engaging with each client. My philosophy is the client has the innate power within themselves to achieve whatever they envision however sometimes we need the power of coaching to be able to see this for ourselves and develop tools to enable the emergence of long term change and self-evolution.
